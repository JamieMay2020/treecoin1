Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TreeCoin

How does TreeCoin actually plant trees?
+
All revenue generated from PumpFun trading fees is automatically donated to verified tree-planting charities like Trees for the Future, One Tree Planted, and local reforestation organizations. We provide full transparency with monthly donation reports.
Is this just another meme coin?
+
While TreeCoin embraces meme culture, we're building real environmental impact. Unlike typical meme coins, 100% of our platform revenue goes to charitable causes. We're proving that fun and purpose can coexist.
How can I verify trees are being planted?
+
We partner only with certified organizations that provide planting certificates, GPS coordinates, and progress photos. Our website features a live dashboard showing exactly how many trees have been funded and where they're being planted.
What blockchain is TreeCoin on?
+
TreeCoin is built on Solana for its eco-friendly, low-energy consensus mechanism and minimal transaction costs. We chose Solana because it aligns with our environmental values - it uses 99.9% less energy than proof-of-work blockchains.
How do I buy TreeCoin?
+
TreeCoin is available on PumpFun and many other DEX. Simply connect your Solana wallet, swap SOL for TreeCoin, and start making an environmental impact with every trade. Links to purchase are available on our X.
What makes TreeCoin different from other green crypto projects?
+
TreeCoin is action-oriented and transparent. Instead of promising future environmental benefits, we're planting trees NOW with every transaction. Our community votes on projects, and we provide real-time impact tracking. Plus, we keep it fun and accessible!
